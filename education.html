2010 - 2015: PRIMARY SCHOOL

Primary school. UPSR was a national-level examination in Malaysia, administered to Year 6 (Standard 6) student at the end of their primary school education. I started my primary school at SK Bukit Jelutong from age 7 to 12 years old. I also take my UPSR at this school and got 2A's, 1B's, 1C's, and 1D's.

2016 - 2020: HIGHSCHOOL

Highschool. For highschool, I continue my studies at SMK Bukit Jelutong and take both PT3 and SPM at this school. PT3 is an assessment that covers the scope of the syllabus from Form 1 to Form 3 based on the aims, objectives and content of the subject. For this, I have got 1A's, 3B's, 2C's, 1D's, 2E's, and 1F's. SPM is a national examination set for all fifth-form secondary school students. At the end of form 4, suddenly we were having a lockdown which forced us to only do online classes for almost 1 year. My batch became the first batch that sat for SPM during COVID-19. Because of this, our SPM dates had been postponed a few times which ended up us being stayed in form 5 for 2 years. For SPM, I managed to get 3B's, 2C+'s, 1C's, 1D's, and 1E's.

2022 - 2024: FORM 6

Form 6. STPM is the Malaysian Higher School Certificate, an advanced-level pre-university examination equivalent to the GCE A-level. It is conducted by the Malaysian Examinations Council (Majlis Peperiksaan Malaysia, MPM) and is typically taken by students in Form 6 after completing secondary school (SPM). For this, I take STPM for 3 sem at Kolej Tingkatan Enam Shah Alam and managed to get 2.42 for my CGPA.

2024: DEGREE

UiTM. I was given the chance to continue my studies in UiTM Puncak Perdana for Degree in Information System Management with 6 semester. Now I am in my second sem with hopes to continue my studies further.
